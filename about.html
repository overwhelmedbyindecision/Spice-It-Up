About Us

We are a team of junior web developers from a variety of backgrounds. Through Spice it Up, we hope to bring our users recipes from all over the world. Spice it Up was created using HTML, Javascript, and Bulma CSS. Using Spoontacular and Calendarific APIs, Spice it Up can get users inspired to change up their routines by providing random recipes, recipes from new cultures, and recipes to fit a variety of dietary restrictions. We hope you enjoy your meal!
